# Contributing

Thanks for helping shape the Open OCPP Trace Format. Feedback, issues, and pull
requests are welcome, especially from people who build or maintain OCPP tooling.

## Licensing of contributions

By contributing you agree that your contributions are licensed under the terms
that apply to this repository (see the README `License` section): specification
text under CC-BY-4.0, and the schema, fixtures, and conformance tests under
Apache-2.0.

## Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO)

Contributions are accepted under the Developer Certificate of Origin
(https://developercertificate.org), a lightweight statement that you have the
right to submit the work under the licenses above. There is no CLA.

Certify it by adding a `Signed-off-by` line to each commit, with a real name and
email:

```
Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>
```

`git commit -s` adds this automatically. The rationale is recorded in ADR-0001
(`docs/adr/0001-licensing.md`).
